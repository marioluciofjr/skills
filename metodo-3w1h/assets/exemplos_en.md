# 3W1H Prompt Practical Examples (English)

These examples demonstrate how to structure prompts using the strict division of the **3W1H** (*Who, What, Where, How*) methodology.

---

### Example 1: Teacher Screaming
- **Who:** A 42-year-old Black female teacher with voluminous afro hair dyed blue. She wears an oversized denim jacket over a bright yellow dress and large gold hoop earrings.
- **What:** The character is screaming an order. Her mouth is wide open, exposing her teeth and tongue. The muscles around her mouth are visibly tense due to the vocal effort.
- **Where:** The image takes place in a studio with a neutral background, such as white or gray, to focus entirely on the character.
- **How:** Professional and realistic photo style. The lighting is sharp studio lighting, highlighting facial tension and vibrant colors (blue, yellow). The angle is a close-up focused on the face to capture the intensity of the emotion.

---

### Example 2: Elderly Man in Rice Terrace (China)
- **Who:** An elderly East Asian man with a wrinkled face and a thin white beard. He wears a dark blue silk tunic (changshan) and a conical bamboo hat (dǒulì).
- **What:** The man is standing silently with a serene and contemplative expression, looking at the horizon.
- **Where:** The scene takes place on a rice terrace in rural China during dawn, with a light mist covering the mountains in the background.
- **How:** Realistic photographic style. The lighting is soft and natural (diffuse dawn light). The angle is a medium shot, capturing the man from the waist up, highlighting the texture of his clothing and the depth of the scenery.

---

### Example 3: Indigenous Weaver
- **Who:** An Indigenous woman with strong features. She has straight black hair held back by a beaded headband on her forehead. She wears a suede tunic with fringes and a turquoise necklace.
- **What:** She is sitting on the ground, weaving a small basket with natural fibers. Her hands are focused on the work, and her expression is serious and concentrated.
- **Where:** The scene occurs at the entrance of a traditional dwelling, overlooking an arid canyon in the background, under a deep blue sky at dusk.
- **How:** Realistic photo style, almost documentary. The lighting is natural and warm ("golden hour" light), highlighting skin tones, the turquoise color, and the suede texture. The angle is a medium shot, capturing her from the waist up, focusing on her hands and expression.

---

### Example 4: Golden Retriever
- **Who:** A large, robust Golden Retriever dog. It has a double, dense, wavy coat of bright golden color. It has floppy ears, a wide snout, and a long, furry tail.
- **What:** The dog is running at high speed toward the camera, with ears flapping in the wind, tongue out, and an alert, happy expression.
- **Where:** The scene takes place in a wide, green grassy park with blurred trees in the background under a clear blue sky.
- **How:** Professional action shot style. The lighting is bright natural daylight. The angle is low-angle to emphasize the dog's size. The capture features a fast shutter speed to freeze motion, with a light bokeh background.

---

### Example 5: Victorian Porcelain Teapot (Still Life)
- **Who:** A Victorian-style porcelain teapot. It has a white background with a delicate floral pattern (pink roses and green leaves). The spout is thin and curved, and the handle is ornamented with faded gold details. A small chip is visible on the rim of the lid.
- **What:** The teapot is resting on a surface, positioned as the central focus of a still life composition.
- **Where:** The scene takes place on a dark wooden table, atop a small lace doily. The background is a living room with soft lighting, perhaps near a window.
- **How:** Realistic photo style, emphasizing "still life." The lighting is soft and diffuse, coming from a side source (window light), highlighting the porcelain texture, the gold shine, and the imperfection of the chip. The angle is a close-up, with a slightly blurred background (bokeh).
